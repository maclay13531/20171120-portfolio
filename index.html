Hello! I'm Jong Park,

I am an universal engineer who loves to build. I moved to the USA when I was 14. After highschool, I attended Florida State University, where I graduated with master's degree in structural engineering. Upon graduating, I joined a design firm as a structural engineer to display my problem-solving skills. For many years, I was fortunate enough to design various buildings such as hospitals and manufacturing facilities that had immediate impact for society

While engineering many projects, I found myself spending more time with computer software that assisted engineers to finish the daily tasks. The growth rate of this software was unbelievably rapid. That's when it hit me and I said to myself, "I want to be part of that rapid growth! I want to be the one to engineer that growth!" Without wasting any time, I dove right into the tech world. I will be graduating from DigitalCrafts' four month, full-time immersive coding bootcamp in January of 2018.
