Hello! I'm Jong Park,

I am an universal engineer who loves to build. I moved to USA when I was 14 then started my new life chapter as a highschooler. After highschool, I attended Florida State University, where I graduated with master's degree in strucutral engineering. Upon graduating, I joined a design firm to display my problem solving skill. For many years, I was fortunate enough to design various buildings such as hospitals and manufacturing facilities that had immediate impact for society.

While engineering many projects, I found myself spending more time with computer softwares that assisted engineers to finish the needed tasks and the growth rate of these softwares were unbelievably rapid. That's when it hit me and I said to myself, "I want to be part of that rapid growth! I want to be the one to engineer that growth!"

Without wasting no time, I dove right into tech world and I will be graduting from DigitalCrafts' four month, full-time immersive coding bootcamp in January of 2018.
